Following the release of our Trail Running Backpack sewing pattern, your number one request was a larger pack for overnight trips. Sporting an alpine climbing aesthetic and our proven vest-style suspension, the Adventure Series Fastpack aims to please.

Features

Pack Features

  • Modern vestlike suspension with soft flask stretch pockets
  • Flexible frame sheet and dual use foam sit pad
  • Removable webbing hip belt compatible with climbing harness
  • Boobies friendly sternum strap spacing in proven vest design
  • Quick access cinch collar with removable lid pocket
  • 24L volume alpine climbing style pack with streamline taper
  • Finished pack measures approx 23" x 10" x 6.5"

Pattern

Downloadable PDF sewing pattern includes

  • Print at home pattern suitable for both Letter and A4 sized paper
  • Large format pattern for 36" roll feed or A0 sized paper
  • Detailed step-by-step sewing instructions with illustrations
  • Custom torso sizing with simple directions and minimal modifications
  • Complete list of materials for a budget build
  • Video sewalong series showing start-to-finish of a fresh build

Two Variations for Shoulder Strap Construction

  • Vest-style straps with soft flask water bottle pockets
  • S-straps with stretch pockets for snacks and InReach
