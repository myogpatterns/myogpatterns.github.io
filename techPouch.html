Features

Travel Ready Design

Upgrade your everyday carry (EDC) or travel kit with this versatile, structured Tech Pouch, tailored for outdoor explorers and tech-savvy travelers. Its compact yet spacious clamshell layout provides exceptional organization, whether you're storing portable electronics or travel essentials.

Inside, the thoughtful design includes a secure zippered pocket and accordion divider for clear compartmentalization, allowing quick access to your gear. Stretch pockets keep tech items or toiletries snug, while a pen loop and slip pockets offer handy storage for small necessities like pens, snacks, or hand sanitizer. With dimensions of 9" x 6" x 3", this pouch blends a sharp, streamlined aesthetic with customizable storage to ensure your essentials stay secure and organized.

  • Adaptable Pocket Layout: Configure the pockets to suit your gear, giving every item a designated spot.
  • Clamshell Access: Opens fully for effortless packing and retrieval of your essentials.
  • Secure Internal Organization: Includes a zippered pocket, accordion divider, stretch pockets, and pen loop for maximum versatility.
  • Modern, Streamlined Look: Make with pack fabrics blending rugged durability with a modern, polished look.
  • Multi-Purpose Design: Use as a tech organizer, utility case, travel dopp kit, or admin pouch.
  • Pattern Versatility: Adjust the materials and features for personalized performance and style.

Designed with adaptability in mind, this pattern lets you create a durable, stylish organizer to meet your unique needs, making it the perfect companion for urban commutes, outdoor adventures, and everything in between.

Pattern Difficulty

This project is designed for intermediate to experienced sewists who are ready to take on a more advanced challenge. While the instructions are detailed, they do not illustrate every stitch or technique, so prior experience with sewing and LearnMYOG patterns like the Everyday Fanny Pack, Porter Pony, or Bikepacking Bags is helpful.

The project involves working with multiple thick layers, sewing tight corners, and using a zipper foot to achieve a clean finish. Seam binding is recommended for added durability and a polished appearance. If you're new to gear sewing or want a simpler project to start with, the Everyday Fanny Pack pattern is an excellent introduction to LearnMYOG designs.

With patience, precision, and attention to detail, you'll be able to craft a professional-quality organizer tailored to your needs!

Materials

The Tech Pouch requires a modest material list, making it an excellent add-on project for those who have completed the Porter Pony or Trail Running Backpack. It's also a fantastic handmade gift idea for outdoor enthusiasts or tech-savvy travelers. To explore material options in depth, check out our Fabrics Guide.

  • Less than a yard of shell fabric: Durable options like Cordura 500D, X-PAC VX21, or EPX200 offer weather resistance and structure.
  • Lightweight lining fabric: Choose bright, moisture-resistant fabrics like 200D Oxford Nylon or PU Coated Ripstop to enhance interior visibility and durability.
  • Lycra mesh for stretch pockets: Adds flexibility and secure storage for small items like cables or power banks without adding bulk.
  • YKK #5 coil zipper: Provides smooth functionality with long-lasting dependability.
