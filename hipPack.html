Adventure Series Hip Pack

#MYOG Sewing Pattern for Bikepacking and Hiking

Bikepackers in the know, have made the switch. Riding backpack-free is not only more comfortable, but also improves mobility. Our Adventure Series Hip Pack sewing pattern is great fitting and highly customizable to fit your style on and off the trail.

Features

  • Designed for riders but adapts equally well to all adventures on and off pavement
  • Capacity without compromise, its wide and rides low for comfort
  • Floating hip belt wings self adjusts to riding posture
  • Customizable pack body for gear inserts, pockets, and attachments
  • Coffin-style lid for ease of access and visibility in the pack
  • Finished pack measures approx 12.5" x 5.5" x 4" for about 4.5L capacity

Pattern includes instructions and material lists for 8 custom variations

  • Laminated foam or breathable mesh back panel
  • Stretch or secure zipper pocket
  • Belt wings and hardware options
  • Gear loops, Load lifters, and compression straps
  • Internal organizer inserts and linings
