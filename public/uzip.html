U-Zip Bag

Features

Bag Features

Inspired by expedition duffel bags this small storage bag originated as an exercise in sewing the U-shaped zipper. The elliptical shape and expansive top lid means clear visibility and access to the contents. With optional overlay panels for durability, U-Zip Bags are optimal for garage tools, climbing hardware, or any other heavy duty use.

  • Wide opening U-Shaped Zipper lid
  • Elliptical profile for free standing structure
  • Optional overlays on bottom and sides
  • Bag measures ~ 9 x 5 x 7 inches (23 x 12.7 x 17.8 cm)
