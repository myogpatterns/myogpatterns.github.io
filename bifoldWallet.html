Bifold Wallet

Slim, tough, and ready for wherever you roam.

A simple bifold card wallet designed for modern materials and fast construction. This pattern uses folding and bonding to create clean edges, structured pockets, and a slim profile without bulky seams. It is an approachable build with a polished result, ideal for everyday carry or gifting.

Choose from three sizes optimized for your carry:

  • 4-card for minimal setups
  • 6-card for balanced daily use
  • 8-card for higher capacity without excess bulk

Sewing Skill Level - Beginner

This is a beginner-friendly project with a focus on folding and bonding rather than complex sewing. The construction is straightforward, with minimal pieces and simple topstitching. Basic accuracy with cutting, pressing, and alignment will lead to a clean result. Ideal for first-time makers working with technical fabrics or anyone looking for a quick, satisfying build.
