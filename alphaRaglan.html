Features

Alpha Raglan Hoodie
  • Minimalist pullover design with a modern style and fitted hood
  • Raglan sleeves with deep shoulder seams to avoid shoulder strap discomfort
  • Two hood styles included with or without cinch cord - balaclava and open neck
  • Designed for Polartec® Alpha® Direct for high breathability and great warmth-to-weight
  • Other great fabric options for a midlayer include fleece and Polartec® Power Grid™
Sewing Skill Level - Confident Beginner
Many confident beginners have successfully shown Alpha Raglan Hoodie to be a perfect entry into MYOG apparel. For best results, a serger is recommended. A zig zag stitch will work but results may vary based on individual skill. This is a great beginner garment pattern. Fleece is forgiving so make a prototype and have fun!
Body Measurements and Fit Guide
Detailed instructions on getting a great fit are included. Sizing covers men's XXS to XL. Two fits are provided per size - a slim active fit and a regular fit. Shown in the gallery, the orange hoodie I'm (6'0" tall, 170 lbs, 39" chest) wearing is a size 39 in Alpha Direct 4028. The black hoodie is the regular fit in Alpha Direct 4008.
Notional custom fit adjustments are illustrated. Womens standard sizing is easily achieved by grading between sizes.

Fabrics

  • Alpha Direct Style 4008, 120 gsm performs as a cold weather midlayer
  • Alpha Direct Style 4024, 186 gsm for extreme warmth
