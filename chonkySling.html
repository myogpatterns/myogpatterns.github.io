Chonky Sling

Design Features

When the venerable Everyday Fanny Pack just won't do, reach for the Chonky Sling.

  • Symmetrical shape and removable strap for ambidextrous wear
  • 3D front zipper pocket and internal organization pockets
  • Inspired strap system self compresses bag contents without fiddly adjusters or hooks
  • External bottle pocket so you don't have to dig through all your stuff
  • Foam lined side, bottom, and back panels for structure and padding precious cargo
  • Thicc boy main bag with over 6L capacity with additional volume in front and bottle pockets
  • Go wild with internal organizers, cell dividers, or external attachments

Sewing Skill Level - Intermediate to Advanced

Chonky Sling is a challenging project as you'll be sewing zippers with linings, darts, adding floating foam panels between layers, sewing through webbing, and precisely sewing curved seams. A heavy duty sewing machine will be needed. The bag can of course be adapted or simplified to suit your skill level and tools. While the instructions detail every step, familiarity with sewing terms and construction techniques is expected. If comfortable making the Everyday Fanny Pack and/or the Bikepacking Frame Bag set, the Chonky Sling provides a nice progression in skill.
