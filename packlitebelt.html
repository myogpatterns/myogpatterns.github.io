Design Features

Your Best-Fitting Running Belt

The Packlite Belt is a precision-fit, low-profile running band designed for both long trail efforts and everyday training runs. This MYOG pattern includes two optimized variations:

  • Trail Belt: A full-featured belt with a removable foam back panel for added structure and soft flask support, ideal for long outings and variable terrain.
  • Training Belt: A streamlined option without the foam insert, slightly narrower at the back for comfort and simplicity during daily runs.

Made for athletes who value comfort, performance, and minimal bounce, the Packlite Belt is constructed from technical stretch fabrics like Lycra mesh or wicking knits such as Polartec Power Dry. It sits comfortably against the skin and can be worn discreetly under a t-shirt or over a light layer, supporting any distance with minimal bulk.

Materials

Fabrics & Notions

The Packlite Belt is to be sewn with knit fabrics with 30-40% stretch. This section provides a quick overview of what's required. Full details, yardage charts, and vendor links are included in the pattern download.

  • Belt Mesh Fabric (1 yd): Lycra Stretch Mesh or Power Mesh
  • Breathable, Wicking Shell (1 yd): Breathable, moisture wicking performance 30-40% stretch fabric that is comforable against the skin. Polartec Power Dry, knit jersey, "dry fit" tshirt fabric, etc. A well-loved race tshirt is a great option.
  • Notions:
    • 3 yds of 3/4 - 1" (20-25mm) Fold Over Elastic (FOE) for belt binding.

    • 3-4mm Closed Cell Foam sheet approximately 12" x 5" (30.5 x 12.7 cm)

    • Mitten hook for the optional key loop
