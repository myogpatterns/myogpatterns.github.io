Features

Purpose Built

A refined toiletry organizer for frequent travelers who value clean construction, smart storage, and a professional finish.

The geometric tapered shape adds structure and a modern look without bulky padding, packing easily into your Porter Pony while holding everyday essentials.

A fully lined interior with minimal bound seams creates a polished result that feels more like technical gear than a basic zipper pouch. It is a satisfying project that builds useful skills and becomes a go-to travel piece.

The interior layout is designed for real use. Stretch zipper pockets keep small items contained and visible, while hidden elastic slip pockets underneath hold bottles, tubes, and grooming tools upright.

An open center compartment stores bulkier items like glasses, deodorant, chargers, and other travel necessities, making packing faster and more organized.

Pattern Difficulty

This pattern is designed for sewists who are ready to move beyond basic flat projects and simple pouches and want to build a more structured, functional piece of gear. You should feel comfortable sewing consistent seam allowances, stitching gentle curves, working through multiple fabric layers, and using a zipper foot.

Along the way, you will learn techniques commonly used in travel and pack construction. These include installing zippers into shaped openings, building layered pocket systems, working with stretch mesh and elastic, constructing a bagged lining, and binding interior seams for a clean, professional finish.

The instructions walk through each step in detail and are supported by a full sew along video, making this a great first project for stepping into more advanced bag making while still being approachable and achievable.

Materials

The Dopp Kit requires typical pack making materials, making it an excellent add-on project for those who have completed the Porter Pony or Tech Pouch. At 9.5 inch long, 3 inch wide, and 5.5 inch tall, it fits easily in a pack or carry on while still holding daily essentials. To explore material options in depth, check out our Fabrics Guide.

  • Less than a yard of shell fabric: Durable options like Cordura 500D, X-PAC VX21, or EPX200 offer weather resistance and structure.
  • Lightweight lining fabric: Choose bright, moisture-resistant fabrics like 200D Oxford Nylon or PU Coated Ripstop to enhance interior visibility and durability.
  • Lycra Power Mesh for stretch pockets: Adds flexibility and secure storage for loose travel-sized essentials.
  • YKK #5 coil zippers: Provides smooth functionality with long-lasting dependability.
